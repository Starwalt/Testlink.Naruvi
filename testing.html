Anaesthesiology

Department of Anaesthesiology

The Department of Anaesthesiology is equipped to provide state-of-the-art safe Anaesthesia and best possible care to all patients in the operating rooms for all routine specialities and subspecialties. As a mandatory prerequisite for the administration of Anaesthesia, all patients will be thoroughly evaluated in the Pre-Anaesthetic Clinic (PAC) on referral from other departments prior to surgery for risk stratification, optimum preoperative preparation and for implementation of risk reduction measures.

Technology & Facilities

  • Routine intraoperative monitoring
  • Special monitoring modalities like entropy, BIS, cerebral oximetry and transesophageal echocardiography are also available where required 
  • Ultrasound-guided regional analgesia
  • Image-guided pain procedures

Services

  • Anaesthesia services in the operation theatres for various sub-specialities and super specialities
  • Pre-anaesthetic clinic (PAC)
  • Anaesthesia services outside the operation theatres in the Radiology suite, Endoscopy suite, Cath lab, Tracheobronchial Endoscopy suite and the Birthing suite (labour analgesia)
  • Pain Medicine
    • Management of chronic pain
    • Acute pain services
    • Translational pain service for immediate postop pain and also prolonged pain after surgery
    • Various procedures on offer include pharmacological, interventional procedures like pain site and nerve injections, radiofrequency ablation, ultrasound-guided musculoskeletal procedures, regenerative therapies, etc.

BIOCHEMISTRY

Department of BIOCHEMISTRY

The Biochemistry Laboratory is committed to providing precise and accurate laboratory results for the early diagnosis, confirmation and monitoring/treatment of diseases. The laboratory offers a wide spectrum of tests, profiles and a variety of test combinations to help our patients and the treating physicians. The laboratory has been provided with well-qualified specialists and technologists who can handle the state-of-the-art instrument and technology. All the analysers are integrated with the hospital LAN system for the quick and error-free transfer of results from the laboratory to the wards and the treating doctors.

Technology & Facilities

  • We have fully automated systems for the measurements of enzymes by kinetic method, substrate by spectrophotometric method and electrolytes by ion-selective electrode
  • For the measurement of hormones, specific proteins, tumour markers, etc. we have fully automated chemiluminescent system 
  • For the separation of specific proteins we have capillary electrophoresis and for the measurement of glycated haemoglobin a dedicated HPLC system are in place

Services

A wide range of tests are performed in the department:
  • Electrolytes
  • Substrates
  • Enzymes
  • Renal function test
  • Liver function test
  • Hormones
  • Tumour markers
  • Cardiac markers
  • Electrophoresis
  • Glycated haemoglobin
  • Heavy metals
  • Vitamins

CARDIOLOGY

The Department of Cardiology at Naruvi Hospitals equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and powered by a team of dedicated and proficient cardiologists, cardio-thoracic surgeons along with well-trained support staff, functions round the clock to attend all kind of cardiac emergencies.

HEAD AND NECK SURGERY

Head and neck cancers are one of the most common cancers in India. A third of these arise from the oral cavity. They can also affect the larynx, pharynx, paranasal sinuses, salivary glands, thyroid, etc. Head and neck cancers can be cured if diagnosed and treated early. The Head and Neck Surgery team at Naruvi Hospitals is committed to provide best cancer care by means of diagnosis, treatment and rehabilitation with precise skill and state-of-the-art technology.
