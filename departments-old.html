PEDIATRIC INTENSIVE CARE UNIT

DEPARTMENT OF PEDIATRIC INTENSIVE CARE UNIT

The Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU) at Naruvi Hospitals is a highly specialised multidisciplinary team of healthcare professionals, which takes care of seriously ill or injured children.

Scope of Services

  • Children who have presented with life-threatening medical or surgical conditions are stabilised, monitored, administered life-supporting treatment and rehabilitated till they are stable for their transfer to a ward
  • Conditions that commonly cause critical illness in children and are managed by the unit include:
    • − Severe infection
    • − Poisoning
    • − Drug overdose
    • − Trauma
    • − Major surgery
    • − Congenital anomalies
  • Our unit is equipped with state- of- art ventilators, monitors and other technology to help monitor and treat children with complex life-threatening conditions
  • The child and the family are at the focal point of care in our unit
  • Strict infection control policies are the cornerstone of our care, and therefore, rigorous steps are taken to ensure the same
  • Our highly committed team strives to offer evidence-based treatment with high standards of care to improve survival, speed recovery, minimise disability and relieve pain and suffering in a caring and respectful manner
