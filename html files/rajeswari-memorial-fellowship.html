Rajeswari Memorial Fellowship

NARUVI HOPITALS announces the 'Rajeswari Memorial Fellowship in Public Health' in loving memory of the late mother of Mr. G V Sampath, Chairman & Founder of Naruvi Hospitals, to foster high quality Public Health Research in India.

This fellowship is to be awarded yearly on a competitive basis to Indian candidates only. This is an educational fellowship, renewable annually, based on performance reviews by the Naruvi Hospitals Academic Council.

The fellowship programme will support research for one or two suitable candidates for a maximum of three years, with a sum of 600,000 INR.

The candidates should have outstanding interest and a track record in public health, with related field experience. The candidate could be registered for a doctoral degree , although not a requirement.

Applicants should have a medical degree from a credible medical school in India or overseas (MCI recognised) or a Masters in Public Health. All documents have to be notarized.

Please send in your application with supporting documents to paulhenry@naruvihospitals.com on or before 31st March 2018.
