Anaesthesiology

Department of Anaesthesiology

Scope of services

  • Anaesthesia services in the operation theatres for various sub-specialities and super specialities
  • Pre-anaesthetic clinic (PAC) 
  • Anaesthesia services outside the operation theatres in the Radiology suite, Endoscopy suite, Cath lab, tracheobronchial Endoscopy suite and the Birthing suite (labour analgesia)
  • Pain Medicine
    • − Management of chronic pain
    • − Acute pain services
    • − Translational pain service for immediate postop pain and also prolonged pain after surgery
    • − Various procedures on offer include pharmacological, interventional procedures like pain site and nerve injections, radiofrequency ablation, ultrasound-guided musculoskeletal procedures, regenerative therapies etc.

Biomedical Engineering

Department Of Biomedical Engineering

Scope of services

  • Naruvi Hospitals boasts of a skilled engineering team that supports clinical staff through effective and seamless use of healthcare technology
  • The team supports the hospital in medical equipment planning, budgeting, evaluation, procurement, installation, training, service and repair, safety and quality checks to ensure the equipment is consistently safe to use for diagnosis and/or treatment
  • To provide timely and cost-effective quality service to clinical department end-users in a professional and responsible manner thereby improving and enhancing patient care by supporting all aspects of medical technology related to patient care
