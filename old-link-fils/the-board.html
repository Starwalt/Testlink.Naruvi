The Board

Chairman G V Sampath

Mr. G V Sampath holds a master’s degree in Commerce and is currently pursuing his doctorate​​ in the area of International Business. He was a trustee of the Vellore Institute of Technology and former Vice President of the VIT University. He was the main driving force behind its enormous expansion. His futuristic thinking, wide range of contacts in and outside the State and Central Governments and a deep commitment to the cause, led to the scaling up of the student strength ten fold, in just over a decade. He was jointly involved in securing international tie-ups for the VIT University, with the best Universities and organizations the world over. History repeats itself in Naruvi. He is responsible for the technical and operational tie-up between Henry Ford Health Systems in Detroit, Michigan, US and the Naruvi Group of Hospitals, the maiden voyage for Henry Ford Health Systems in India, and a unique model for our country.

He is also the Managing trustee of SANCO Foundation, a Public Charitable Trust, besides being the Chairman and Managing Director of M/S Pearl Human Care Private Limited and M/S Gold Land Ventures Private Limited. He is also the Managing Partner of M/S Pearl Infra Works LLP and M/S Onyx Trading, which are into cement and steel trading.

He has vast experience in exports and administration, as Managing Director of a 100% export oriented granite unit some years ago. He held the position of Vice President of the Indian Monument and Manufacturers Association as well.

Executive Director Dr. Paul Henry

Dr. Paul Henry, a graduate and post-graduate in Neurosurgery from the esteemed Christian Medical College, Vellore, was active in intensive teaching and surgical assignments in India and overseas.

He has had a very successful career as a Neurosurgeon for the past two decades spanning across countries like Saudi Arabia, Bangladesh etc., apart from serving as a Neurosurgeon in reputed Indian hospitals like CMCH Vellore, and as a Senior Consultant in Apollo and MIOT Hospitals.

His commitment to the cause of setting up a premier super-speciality hospital, boasting the highest levels of excellence, has prompted him to take up this current assignment as full time Executive Director of Naruvi.

Advisory Board

Mr. K Krishnan, our Chief Advisor, is a Chartered Accountant by profession, with over 35 years spent in large corporates like the Murugappa Group, RPG Group and Airtel. He is a seasoned team leader, who proved his mettle, setting up large green field ventures, building new organisations and successfully running large operations. He was joint president of Airtel, when he retired, to set up his own organisation.

Padma Bhushan Dr. K I Varaprasad Reddy is renowned all over the country as an eminent entrepreneur, and has carved a niche for himself in the areas of research and industry. He founded Shantha Biotechnics, the first company to develop, manufacture and market recombinant human healthcare products in India.

Mr. Ravindra Sanna Reddy is the Founder and Managing Director of Sri City - the largest integrated business city in South India. It is home to over 108 globally known units from nearly 26 countries. Companies like Isuzu, Cadbury, PepsiCo, Alstom, Colgate Palmolive, Danieli, Kellogg’s, Kobelco and many others adorn the Sri City premises.

Mr. CK Ranganathan is the founder and Managing Director of M/S CavinKare Pvt. Ltd. The Company manufactures and markets numerous products under brands such as Chik, Spinz, Fairever, Meera, Nyle, Raaga Professional, Karthika, Indica, Ruchi,Chinnis, Maa, Garden, Cavins etc., both for domestic and international markets.

Mr. Abid Junaid is the MD of The Radiant Star Group, Dubai, a newly established conglomerate, comprising of Real Estate, Construction and Facility Management verticals, with branches across the UAE and India. The company is involved in developing real estate in prime areas of Dubai totalling over one million square foot. In addition, they have several projects in the pipeline too.
